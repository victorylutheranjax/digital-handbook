# Website Access

Use this guide to access Victory's new website. Click on the images to expand them.

## WordPress Dashboard

1. Go to [sharefaith.com](https://sharefaith.com).

2. Click "Login" in the upper left.

   ![An arrow pointing to the login button on the ShareFaith homepage](https://www.victorylutheran.org/hp_wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/1_homepage.webp){.shadow-img}

3. Enter the login details and click "Login".

   ![An arrow pointing to the login button on the ShareFaith login page](https://www.victorylutheran.org/hp_wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/2_login.webp){.shadow-img}

4. Click the "Website" card on the ShareFaith account dashboard.

   ![An arrow pointing to the website card on the ShareFaith account dashboard](https://www.victorylutheran.org/hp_wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/3_website.webp){.shadow-img}

5. Click "Edit Your Website".

   ![An arrow pointing to the "Edit Your Website" button on the ShareFaith account dashboard](https://www.victorylutheran.org/hp_wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/4_edit.webp){.shadow-img}

6. Click "Draft Site".

   ![An arrow pointing to the "Draft site" button on the ShareFaith account dashboard](https://www.victorylutheran.org/hp_wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/5_draft-site.webp){.shadow-img}
   ::: info Note
   If you are prompted by WordPress to login again, use the same credentials as you used to log into ShareFaith.
   :::

## Website Frontend

1. In the sidebar, click "Pages".
   ::: info Note
   On mobile, you may have to click "Pages" then "All Pages".
   :::
   ![An arrow pointing to the "Pages" item in the WordPress dashboard sidebar](https://www.victorylutheran.org/hp_wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/6_dashboard.webp){.shadow-img}

2. Find the page you wish to visit and hover over its table row.

3. Click "View" to view the page as a visitor or "Edit with Elementor" to edit the page.

   ![Two arrows pointing to the "View" and "Edit with Elementor" options on the "Pages" view of the WordPress dashboard](https://www.victorylutheran.org/hp_wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/7_pages.webp){.shadow-img}
